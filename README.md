
# FUTURE_DS_01 – Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing retail sales data to identify revenue trends, product performance, customer segments, and regional profitability. The goal is to generate actionable business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

## Objectives

- Analyze sales and profit trends over time
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Evaluate regional and segment-wise performance
- Generate business insights and recommendations
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Power BI

## Key Insights

- Sales showed an overall upward trend during the analysis period.
- Technology emerged as a leading category in terms of sales and profitability.
- The Consumer segment contributed the highest share of revenue.
- Regional performance varied significantly across markets.
- Top-performing products generated a substantial share of total profit.

## Recommendations

- Increase focus on high-performing categories and regions.
- Optimize inventory planning during peak-demand periods.
- Review pricing and discount strategies for low-margin products.
- Strengthen marketing efforts targeting profitable customer segments.
